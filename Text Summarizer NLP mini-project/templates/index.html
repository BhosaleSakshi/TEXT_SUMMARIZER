Extractive Summarization

  • Selects the most important sentences or phrases from a text to create a summary.
  • Involves using algorithms to identify important sentences based on factors like frequency and position.
  • Summary may lacks coherence or does not follow the flow of content well.
  • Commonly used in news aggregation, academic contexts and news articles summary.
  • Can be performed using simple, traditional algorithms and machine learning techniques
